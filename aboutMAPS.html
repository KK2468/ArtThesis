Hi, I'm Kitty! Thanks for visiting my site!

This interactive map I put together explores a few of the many neighborhoods in Brooklyn, NYC, namely in towards the south and western coastlines, where I live.

This project was actually inspired by a really long walk my friend and I went on one summer, from the Brooklyn Bridge area all the way down to Coney Island. It took a good 10 hours or so, including detours for food. But we made it to Coney Island just in time for the Friday night fireworks.

I've walked this distance with my mom years before, but from the other direction (starting in the south and going up towards Manhattan). So, it wasn't until this trip that I realized how different the Brooklyn neighborhoods are. The most prominent thing I noticed was this transition from the Manhattan-esque high rises of the northwest Brooklyn to familiar residential buildings and delis. On some of my later walks, I realized that this latter part was what made Brooklyn feel like home to me whereever I went.

So a huge shout out to Yulan for going on this walk with me!

So, I designed this interactive map in hopes that people like YOU can virtually "walk around" Brooklyn and discover some of what I consider to be the lesser known gems of the city.

So just HOVER over or CLICK on a pin on the main map...

...and let the Adventure Begin!
